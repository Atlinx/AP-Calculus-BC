# Ratio Test

Given a series with the $n\text{th}$ term of $a_n$,

$$
\Large
\begin{aligned}
& \lim_{x\to\infty} \left| \frac{a_{n+1}}{a_{n}} \right| = L \\\\

& L > 1 \to \text{diverges} \\
& L < 1 \to \text{converges} \\
& L = 0 \to \text{inconclusive} \\
\end{aligned}
$$
