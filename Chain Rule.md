# Chain Rule

$$\huge \frac{d}{dx} \left[ f(g(x)) \right] = f'(g(x)) \cdot g'(x)$$

> **Ex.**
> $$
\Large
\begin{aligned}
& \frac{d}{dx} \cos(3x^4) \\
& = -\sin(3x^4) \cdot (3 \cdot 4x^3) \\
& = -12x^3\sin(3x^4)
\end{aligned}
> $$

Used to take derivatives of nested functions.
